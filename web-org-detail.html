About

The Muslim Student Association at the University of Washington is the heart of Muslim student life on campus. We host weekly halaqas, Friday Jummah prayers, community iftars during Ramadan, and large-scale events like the Annual MSA Gala.

Our mission is to foster a welcoming, spiritually enriching, and socially active Muslim community that serves both Muslim and non-Muslim students.
